About Me

Hello! I'm Henry Asawo D, a passionate full-stack developer and technology researcher with over 5 years of experience building innovative web applications and conducting cutting-edge research in human-computer interaction.

My expertise includes JavaScript, React, Node.js, Python, and cloud technologies. I've published several research papers on UI/UX optimization and AI-driven development tools in reputable journals.

When I'm not coding or writing, you can find me hiking, reading tech blogs, or experimenting with new programming frameworks. I'm always open to discussing new projects, creative ideas, or research collaborations.

I'm committed to delivering high-quality work and ensuring that every project I undertake exceeds expectations. My approach combines technical expertise with research-backed insights to create solutions that are both functional and delightful to use.

I've worked with startups and established companies across various industries, helping them transform their ideas into successful digital products. My research work has been cited over 200 times in academic literature.
